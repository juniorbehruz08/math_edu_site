1. The Plane (2D Space)

← → ↑ ↑

A plane is a flat, two-dimensional surface that extends infinitely in all directions.

Key properties:

  • Has length and width
  • Has no thickness
  • All 2D geometric figures lie inside a plane

Think of a plane as an ideal flat surface, not a physical object.
