General Information Relating to Fractions

A fraction represents a part of a whole. It is written as numerator/denominator. The numerator (top number) shows how many parts we have, and the denominator (bottom number) shows how many equal parts the whole is divided into.

What are Equivalent Fractions?

Some fractions look different but have the same value. For example, 1/2 and 2/4. Both cover the same half of the circle.
